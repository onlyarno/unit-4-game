crystal collector

Instructions

Random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals. Click crystals to add a specific amount of points to your total score. Win by matching your total score to random number, lose if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will change.
